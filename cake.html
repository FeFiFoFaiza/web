Sponge Cake

It's a plain vanilla cake but spongey. This makes either one or two cakes, depends on the sizes of your pans. These are some tins I found on Amazon. It has a 4.6 rating from 18,796 reviews so I'm sure they're good.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp hot water

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 330Â°F. Grease and line cake tins with parchment paper.
  2. Transfer egg whites to a seperate bowl and add half cup of sugar. Beat the eggs until white and fluffy.
  3. In another bowl with the egg yolks, add the rest of the sugar, oil, vanilla extract and hot water and mix. Sift in the flour and baking powder and mix that too.
  4. Fold in the egg whites one bit at a time. Don't over fold.
  5. Dump it into the tins (gently) and shove it into the hot oven (gently). Cook and monitor for around 17 minutes or until it's done.
