Blueberry Muffin

It's a cupcake with no frosting but with fruit. Sure you can use chocolate chips instead, but why would you do that when you can trick your brain into thinking you're eating healthy. Just look at all the benefits of blueberries!

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/8 tsp baking powder
  • 1/16 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp blueberries , fresh or frozen

Topping

  • 1 tbsp butter, cold and hard like frozen fish
  • 1 1/2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons brown sugar, regular normal old white sugar can work too I guess
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

    • Steps:

    1. In a microwave safe mug mix together the first 6 ingredients until smooth.
    2. Mix in the blueberries.
    3. In a small bowl, rub together the topping ingredients with a fork until butter pieces resemble bread crumbs. And sprinkle that stuff on top of the batter.
    4. Microwave for 1 minute or until it is firm to the touch on top.
