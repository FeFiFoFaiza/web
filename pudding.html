Chocolate Pudding

To be honest, I don't understand what chocolate pudding is. It doesn't taste like chocolate really and it basically is a mousse. But apparently there is a difference between those two?

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp bittersweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup full fat milk

Steps:

  1. In a large microwavable mug mix in the chocolate and cornstarch. Pour in the salt, vanilla and milk and stir
  2. Microwave for 1 minutes, then whisk with a fork. Microwave for another 30 seconds and whisk again. It should starting to thicken depending on your microwave ofcourse.
  3. Continue microwaving for short intervals (15 seconds-ish) until it is nice and thick (always keep a close eye on your mug meals so they don't over heat or over flow)
