Snickerdoodle Mug Cake

Because who needs snickerdoodle cookies when you can just turn it into a mug cake? For some odd reason there's also snickerdoodle bread. I honestly have no idea why that exists. Who does that to bread? That's just horrible.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup + 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tspn baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Topping/Layering

  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and cinnamon until thoroughly, completely combined, with no streaks of any ingredients remaining. Blend in milk, butter, and vanilla until batter is smooth. Into a 14-ounce (or larger) microwave-safe mug with straight sides, scoop a big spoonful of batter, then sprinkle with a spoonful of cinnamon sugar. Alternate layers, ending with cinnamon sugar.
  2. Microwave on high for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes, or until cake is done since all microwaves are unique and have their own quirks like people. Eat it hot and burn your tongue or wait for it to cool down.
